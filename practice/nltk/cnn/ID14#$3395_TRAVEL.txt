JetBlue Airways Corporation

 (CNN) -- Looking for an affordable last-minute getaway? JetBlue Airways is celebrating its 10th birthday with a $10 one-day fare sale Monday on flights from New York to the airline's original 10 destinations. Travel must be booked on JetBlue's Web site by 11:59 p.m. CST Monday for travel on Tuesday or Wednesday, March 9 or 10. Flights included in JetBlue's &quot;Thank You fare&quot; promotion go from the airline's home base at New York's John F. Kennedy International airport to Fort Lauderdale, Tampa, Orlando, West Palm Beach and Fort Myers, Florida; Buffalo and Rochester, New York; Oakland, California; Burlington, Vermont; and Salt Lake City, Utah. The airline plans to roll out a series of monthly deals to mark the beginning of its second decade, a JetBlue announcement said. On Wednesday, JetBlue will announce giveaways for New York-based customers on Twitter, the announcement said. &quot;We are excited to kick things off in our own backyard with these $10 fares and Wednesday's giveaway in New York,&quot; said Robin Hayes, the airline's chief commercial officer, in a statement.
